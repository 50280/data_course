   - **Legislation**: The European Parliament, along with the Council of the EU, has the power to pass EU laws. This is known as the ordinary legislative procedure. The Commission, which is the executive branch, proposes the laws, but the Parliament and Council must approve them.
   - **Budget**: The Parliament has the power to approve or reject the EU's budget.
   - **Supervision**: The Parliament supervises the work of the Commission and can force it to resign through a motion of censure.
